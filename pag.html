Welcome to Lungalunga PAG Church. We are a family of believers passionate about worship, prayer, and the transforming power of God. Our mission is to raise a Spirit-filled generation that serves God wholeheartedly and transforms the community through faith and love.

“Then you call on the name of your god, and I will call on the name of the LORD. The God who answers by fire—He is God.” — 1 Kings 18:24

About Us

Lungalunga Pentecostal Assemblies of God (PAG) is a Christ-centered church committed to prayer, discipleship, and spreading the Gospel.

Mission: To win souls, disciple believers, and impact the community through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Vision: To raise a praying, worshiping, and Spirit-filled generation transforming lives for Christ.

Ministries

  • Men’s Fellowship – Building strong, God-fearing men.
  • Women’s Ministry – Equipping women to serve in faith and love.
  • Youth Ministry – Empowering young people to lead in purity and purpose.
  • Teens Ministry – Guiding teens in their identity and faith journey.
  • Children’s Ministry (Sunday School) – Nurturing children in God’s Word through songs, stories, and activities.
  • Praise & Worship Team – Leading the church into God’s presence.
  • Outreach & Evangelism – Spreading the message of Christ to the community.
  • Intercessory Team – Standing in the gap in prayer for the church and nation.
